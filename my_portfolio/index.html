My Projects

Protection of crops from wild animals using intelligence surveillance

This project aims to protect crops from wildlife intrusions using machine learning algorithms - OpenCV and YOLOv3 to detect animals in real-time. Once an intrusion is detected, the system alerts the farmer through audio notifications. This proactive approach helps farmers mitigate crop damage and promotes coexistence between agriculture and wildlife.

Introducing Responsive TV

A solution promoting healthy screen habits by recognizing individuals and adjusting display settings accordingly. With a focus on maintaining optimal viewing distances, this innovative system automatically powers down when children approach a predetermined distance from the screen, fostering balanced screen time for children.

Sales Prediction

A Flask-based web application leveraging Prophet time series forecasting to predict future sales trends. Users upload CSV data and specify the timeframe (year, week, month, or day) for prediction. The system generates visual graphs and statistical insights, aiding businesses in strategic decision-making.
